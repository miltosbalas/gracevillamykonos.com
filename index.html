Welcome to Grace Villa

Tranquility, Privacy & the Cycladic Light

Located in the peaceful area of Ornos, only a 5-minute walk from Ornos & Korfos beaches and a short ride from cosmopolitan Mykonos Town, Grace Villa is a 138 sq.m. three-level property — part of an exclusive complex of five villas — boasting whitewashed Cycladic architecture, wooden pergolas and sweeping sea views.

The villa accommodates up to 11 guests across five bedrooms, with a Master Suite offering a private balcony and panoramic veranda overlooking the Aegean. Guests enjoy access to a large shared pool, alfresco dining terraces and complete tranquility, while remaining minutes from the island's vibrant nightlife.

  • 5 minutes walk to Ornos & Korfos Beach
  • 5 minutes drive to Mykonos Town & Airport
  • Shared swimming pool & sun terraces
  • Free private parking on site
  • Fully equipped open-plan kitchen
